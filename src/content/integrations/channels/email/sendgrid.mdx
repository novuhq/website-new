<IntegrationSection title="Overview" icon="eye">
SendGrid is an email delivery platform that provides APIs for transactional and marketing email.
</IntegrationSection>

<IntegrationSection title="How it works" icon="refresh-cw">
Novu routes email notifications through your SendGrid account using the SendGrid API. Delivery status is tracked automatically.
</IntegrationSection>

<IntegrationSection title="Configure" icon="settings">
Add your SendGrid API key, verified sender email, and sender name in the Novu dashboard under Integrations.
</IntegrationSection>
