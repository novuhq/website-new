<IntegrationSection title="Overview" icon="eye">
Amazon Simple Email Service (SES) is an email sending service from AWS for transactional and bulk email.
</IntegrationSection>

<IntegrationSection title="How it works" icon="refresh-cw">
Novu authenticates with your AWS credentials and sends email notifications through the SES API.
</IntegrationSection>

<IntegrationSection title="Configure" icon="settings">
Provide your AWS access key, secret key, region, sender name and verified sender email.
</IntegrationSection>
