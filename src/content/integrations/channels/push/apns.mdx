<IntegrationSection title="Overview" icon="eye">
APNS is Apple's service for delivering push notifications to iOS, macOS, and Safari.
</IntegrationSection>

<IntegrationSection title="How it works" icon="refresh-cw">
Novu routes push notifications through APNS using device tokens.
</IntegrationSection>

<IntegrationSection title="Configure" icon="settings">
Provide your APNS key, key ID, team ID, and bundle ID.
</IntegrationSection>
